Grading

  • Labs: 75%

    Labs will be submitted via email. There will be roughly one lab per class. Labs should be turned in anytime before the last day of classes.

  • Final Coding Project: 20%

    Students can work in a group or alone. Topic can be chosen by student. I will also provide a list of project ideas. Details to come.

  • Course Surveys: 5%

    Periodically, I will give technical surveys to check student comprehension. These will not be graded. However, the top ten scoring students will recieve ten bonus points added to their final project grade.
